House Party
Due to public health and social measures put in place by the novel CoronaVirus, I spent most of my fall semester at home. While these restrictions are dire, I can’t help feel down about the lively college parties I’m missing out on. I decided to take matters into my own hands. With the magic of Augmented Reality, I created my own party and had a blast with new friends (yeah, without masks!).
